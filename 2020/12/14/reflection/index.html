Final Reflection

Posted on 2020-12-14

the writing class is coming to an end.
Because of the covid-19, instead of having classes at school,I can only take online classes and upside down the day and night at home.

thanks to ead, the course is asynchronous.

In this class, I learned many interesting topics about racial problem. I think I know it very well because the professor has provided us with many articles on these subjects. I don’t think my understanding of issue of that has changed. Just as I think, racial discrimination is very close to us. No matter in any country, even in China, which is composed of the majority of the Han people, there will still be all kinds of discrimination. But people all over the world should develop harmoniously and freely.

What impresses me most is project 1, because it makes me understand myself deeply. In the past, I used to read and write about others, but I didn’t think deeply about myself. Project 4, which is what I’m doing now. Let me learn how to build a website from scratch. Although I’m a step behind others, the professor asked me to simply build a website, but I thought about it seriously, I decided to make a real decision, not just to hand in my homework, but to build a website of my own with my own domain name. In the future, I want to put some of my photography works on my blog.

As a student who take English as secondary language. It is inevitable to write wrong grammar in every assignment. In this case, the feedback of professor would be a excellent approach to improve my writting. Because we need to revise every project. Learn during the revision to avoid the same mistake happen in future.

For Ead II, we need to explore our own idea and contrast with the article. Personally speaking, It does reduce the numbers of words, but limited my thinking. For many times I got inspiration but have no connection with aeticle, so I have to throw it away.

Project 2 must be my challenge, because it take me long time to read the article, and I even don’t understand for some paragraph.

Project 4 must surprised me a lot. because for long time I wish to build a personal blog, but I always consider it would too difficult and spend too many time. Thanks to the project 4, it gives me the motivation to build this blog.
